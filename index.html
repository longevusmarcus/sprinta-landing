There's a marathon you want, and a time you're chasing. Getting there doesn't take more data — it takes coaching that adapts to your life, the discipline that decides races, and a real person accountable for the result.

How it works

01

Choose your race

Pick the marathon and the time you're after. Sprinta tells you, honestly, whether it's within reach this season, or shows you a race that is.

So you start with a goal you can actually trust.

02

Get a plan built for it

A plan worked back from race day and your real fitness, synced automatically with Garmin and Strava, so every run you do is already counted.

So you train for your race, not a generic template.

03

It keeps you honest

Miss a week or pick up a niggle and your plan rebuilds toward race day. And it holds you to easy-easy, hard-hard, so you stop running your easy days too hard and quietly sabotaging the block.

So you do the training that actually works, not just the training you feel like.

04

A human, when it counts

The AI coaches you day to day. For the calls an algorithm shouldn't make, the niggle, the taper, the week it all goes wrong, a certified human steps in alongside you.

So someone is accountable for your result, not just your data.
